WilliamHsu

is a designer based in New York City. Entering his fourth year in Parsons School of Design majoring in Communication Design, he is interested in exploring visual graphics in more forms than one.

His works oftentimes aim to incite internal conversations within the audience, implementing satire and humor in his works. If you’re thinking he is crazy, he is not (depends who’s asking), he just likes to have fun in his work!

    World's Most Famous Hot Dog
    ✶ Motion Graphic, Branding ✶

    The Hot Dog animation is every child’s packaging come to life. The bright red and yellow colors against each other become a simple yet compelling visual motif throughout the adventures of a hot dog wrapper.

    The hot dog wrapper inspiration comes from “Bæjarins Beztu Pylsur” which translates to “The Town’s Best Hot Dogs,” acquired at a layover in the Keflavik airport. The simple and animated design really kickstarted an imaginative scenario in my mind that resulted in this motion graphic that seems like it jumped right out of a state fair.
